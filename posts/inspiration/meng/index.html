Asia Poem: Meng(氓)

2021-05-12 :: ChallenAi #poem  #Asia 

This poem are selected from ‘the story of songs’ which is written in about 300 AC, almost the same time with ‘the art of war’ which is more famous than the former.

Inspiration⌗

The reason I translate this is I read an article (Why Women Sometimes Give Up On Men They Love)[https://psiloveyou.xyz/why-women-will-leave-men-they-love-a320d1e33c44] It remind me of a love story almost 2000 years ago in a totally diffirent culture.

Let’s take a look at this story.

Chapter One⌗

A man looks attractive comes to me, use his cloth to swap my silk.

But he doesn’t want the silk, his target is me.

I’m about to accompany with him to the River Qi when we seperate, but we finally arrived at Hill Dunqu.

I don’t want to delay our marriage inside my heart, but you(the man) never make a formal propose.

Please don’t be angry, I promise you autumn is the time.

Chapter Two⌗

I miss you so sick, I stand in the city wall to look for you, I can feel my smile so bright when I find you, I can also feel my pain if you don’t appear.

You ask for fortune-teller to divine our future, It’s bright, so I enter your carriage and get married without doubt.

Chapter Three⌗

When the leaf of mulbery hasn’t withered, it looks colorful. (When I’m still young, I looks beautiful.)

Turtledove, don’t eat too much mulbery fruit. Gril, don’t fall too deep in love. (Chinese myth says the turtledove will get poisoning when it eats too much mulbery fruit)

If a man fall in love, he can still leave, if a woman fall in love, she will get trapped. (In the traditional society, if a woman has married with someone, she is really hard to remarry with someone else when divorced, the man is totally different)

Chapter Four⌗

Now the leafs of mulbery has withered, they fall down from the limb.

Since I came to your family, my life became poor.

River Qi runs day and night, it wets the curtain of my carriage (when I decide to leave you).

I have no fault in my behavior, but you are inconsistent.

Chapter Five⌗

After I become your wife, I work hard for our family; I go to bed late, I wake up early.

(However, we often have conflict.) Since the verbally abusive ends, the family violence starts.

Brothers are not wise enough, laugh loudly at me.

I stay alone and calm down to consider about myself, how sad I am!

Chapter Six⌗

River Qi looks so board, but it still has its border. Swamp Xi looks so large, but it also has its end. (so do I.)

I still remember my warm family time when I’m a little girl.

Even though once we own the swear and promise from each other, we stand in different sides now, I will never think about you again, it’s end.

mordern situation⌗

The lucky part is nowadays, we can easily devorce without to much limit, especially in the western society.(Asian countries are usually much traditional.) However, marriage is a serious topic, not only about outlooking, money, the same story happens everyday.

When I read the article before, I can feel her pain. But my opinion is don’t give your husband/wife advice to listen to you, or try to change his personality. Instead, find someone really cares about you, natually he will talk and listen to you, about dreams, future.
